# Exploration Rules — Valence Mirage

## Action Types
| Type | Relevant Stat | Default Risk | Default Scale |
|------|--------------|-------------|---------------|
| explore | wisdom | low | moderate |
| investigate | intelligence | low | minor |
| observe | wisdom | low | minor |
| interact | charisma | low | minor |

## Discovery System
- Each area has hidden elements (secrets, items, lore, paths)
- Observation/investigation rolls determine what's found
- Multiple discoveries possible per area with successive checks
- Critical success: reveal hidden major discovery
- Partial success: find something useful but incomplete

## Environmental Hazards
- Traps: dexterity check to avoid, damage on failure
- Difficult terrain: dexterity check, failure = slowed or minor damage
- Cursed areas: wisdom check, failure = status effect
- Locked passages: intelligence or strength check to open

## NPC Encounters
- Social actions (persuade, deceive, intimidate) use charisma
-NPCs have disposition: hostile, neutral, friendly, allied
- Successful social rolls improve disposition
- Failed rolls may worsen disposition or trigger combat
- Key NPCs may have unique interaction rules

## Rest & Recovery
- Short rest: restore 10 HP + 10 mana (costs 1 story beat)
- Long rest: restore to full HP/mana (costs 1 act transition)
- Cannot rest during combat or while enemies are aware
