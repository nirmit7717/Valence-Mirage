# Progression Rules — Valence Mirage

## Experience Points
| Outcome | XP Award |
|---------|----------|
| Critical Success | 30 |
| Success | 20 |
| Partial Success | 15 |
| Failure | 10 |
| Critical Failure | 5 |
| Narrative Choice | 5 |

## Leveling
- Level 1 → 2: 100 XP
- Each subsequent level: XP requirement * 1.5
- Formula: xp_to_next = floor(100 * 1.5^(level-1))

## Level Up Rewards
- +5 Max HP, +5 Max Mana (current also increased by 5)
- +1 to a random core stat
- Campaign milestone unlocks at certain levels (DM discretion)

## Stat System
| Stat | Affects |
|------|---------|
| Strength | Physical actions, melee damage, carrying capacity |
| Intelligence | Spellcasting, investigation, knowledge checks |
| Dexterity | Evasion, stealth, ranged attacks, initiative |
| Control | Mana efficiency, concentration, resisting effects |
| Charisma | Social interactions, persuasion, leadership |
| Wisdom | Perception, insight, survival, healing |

All stats start at 10 (baseline). Range: 1-20.

## Passive Regeneration
- +1 HP per turn (unless critical failure on that turn)
- +1 Mana per turn (unless critical failure on that turn)

## Inventory Limits
- Max 20 items in inventory
- Items can be dropped, traded, or consumed
- Quest items cannot be dropped
- Equipped weapons/armor provide stat bonuses
